sketch_canvas
Not a NuxtUI primitive — a small pointer-based canvas inside NuxtUI chrome. Pen, eraser, undo, clear, two stroke widths. Touch + mouse. Strokes persist as a serialisable array. It serves the protocol, nothing more. This live demo is look-and-feel only — rebuild it natively in Vue, do not port.
